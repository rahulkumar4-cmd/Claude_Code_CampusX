Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling filtering by category or date. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app on your behalf.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and access is restricted to your account. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time, after which it will be permanently removed from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your data with third parties. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting or database services) to operate the app; these providers are contractually bound to handle your data securely and only as directed by us. We do not integrate with advertising networks or analytics platforms that track you across other sites.

5. Cookies

We use a single session cookie to keep you signed in. No tracking cookies or third-party cookies are used. You can clear cookies at any time through your browser settings, which will sign you out of your account.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about how your data is handled, or would like to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out through the app's support channel. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
